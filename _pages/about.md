I am a first-year PhD candidate at the [HKUST NLP Group](https://nlp.ust.hk/), advised by Professor [Junxian He](https://nlp.ust.hk/~jxhe/). Previously, I obtained my B.Eng. from [Shanghai Jiao Tong University](https://www.sjtu.edu.cn/) in June 2024.

## Research Interests

My research focuses on natural language processing and machine learning, with specific interests in:

- **LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning**
- **Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)**
- **LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability**
